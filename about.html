About Me

AKIL

Hi! I'm Akil, a passionate visual designer with a focus on creating graphic design, illustration, and photography. I love the process of bringing ideas to life and exploring the ways visual elements can communicate and evoke emotions. I'm driven by a desire to continually learn and push my creative boundaries, always seeking new techniques and approaches to design.

Currently, I'm a fourth-semester student at Indonesia University of Education, pursuing a degree in Multimedia Education. This academic journey has been invaluable in expanding my knowledge of visual arts and digital media. It's provided me with a strong foundation in design principles and technical skills, while also fostering my critical thinking and creative problem-solving. I'm excited to apply what I've learned to real-world projects and contribute to the dynamic field of visual design.

By the way, I primarily work with Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, Clip Studio Paint, Figma, and CapCut, allowing me to create high-quality visuals across various media formats. Whether it's vector art, photo manipulation, UI/UX design, motion graphics, or video editing. I love experimenting with different tools to bring my creative vision to life.
